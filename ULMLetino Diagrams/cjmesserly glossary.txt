Glossary 
1. Terminal - The device that the health care professional uses to access ChocAn software
2. Accounting Software - The software that manages payment to health care professionals
3. Health Care Professionals - ChocAn registered providers who provide services for members
4. Communications Software - The software that sends service information as well as weekly reports
5. Member - Subscribed member to ChocAn services
6. Suspended Member - Member who has missed a payment but has not terminated their membership
7. Plastic Card - Unique ID card given to all members that is used in validation
8. Validation - The process that guarantees a member is in good financial standing with ChocAn
9. EFT File - File that contains payment information for disbursement
10. Provider Directory - File containing all possible service ID codes in alphabetical order
